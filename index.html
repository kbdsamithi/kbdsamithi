About Us

Serving the Community with Dhamma & Compassion

Karnatak Buddha Dhamma Samithi is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to preserving and promoting Buddhist teachings, culture, and values across Karnataka.

Founded with the vision of creating a harmonious community guided by the Buddha's teachings, we bring together practitioners, scholars, and supporters to spread the timeless message of peace, compassion, and wisdom.
